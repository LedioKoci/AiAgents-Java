APEX DATA-BRIDGE (ADB) - MAINTENANCE POLICY

1. FIRMWARE UPDATES
- Critical security patches are applied automatically between 02:00 and 04:00 local time.
- Feature updates must be manually approved by an admin in the dashboard.
- We recommend checking for updates at least once per month.

2. BACKUP PROCEDURES
- The ADB's internal configuration database is automatically backed up daily at midnight to the configured cloud storage bucket.
- We retain the last 7 daily backups.
- To perform an on-demand backup, use the 'Backup Now' button on the 'System Health' page.

3. CLEANING
- The ADB physical unit has no user-serviceable parts.
- Do not open the chassis.
- Dust can be cleared from the side vents using compressed air. Do not use liquid cleaners.
